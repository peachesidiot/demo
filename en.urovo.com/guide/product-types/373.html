Mobile Computers-For Better Scanning and Management-DT40 and DT50

2021-11-05 14:24:00
Source：Urovo

Urovo Mobile Computers


Urovo’s mobile computers are rugged computers for better scanning and management. They possess built-in advanced scanning engines and high-performance CPU, which prepares prerequisites for superior mobile computers. In this article, we will discuss the Urovo DT 40 & DT 50.



Urovo DT 40--A full keypad durable industrial mobile computer


Urovo DT 40, a rugged mobile computer scanner, features better configuration with Octa-core CPU and Android 9.0 system. We all know that a strong configuration is the basis of smooth operation. Therefore smooth operation and strong configuration become the biggest advantages of this mobile computer scanner DT 40.



What Are the Merits that the Urovo DT 40 Possess?


1. Dual input design-Comprise of a keypad and touch screen input functions.

The mobile computer DT 40 looks dash and the keypad is convenient to tap therefore we don’t have to worry touching by mistake.


2. Fast WiFi seamless roaming -Enable stable connecting internet.

Rapid, seamless WiFi connection allows you to enjoy high-speed internet freely. There are no worries about lost internet connection.


3. Strong sound feedback-The optimized loudspeaker is designed for indoor places, which enables people to hear clear sound even in a noisy environment.


4. Powerful operational performance-Eight core CPU, high operation speed with low consumption.

When the mobile computer scanner is working, it performs with multiple functions while only costing low energy which extends the battery life and maintains at a relatively low heat level.


5. Responsive and accurate code scanning-Adopts international professional 2D module

Responsive time only takes milliseconds, providing fast scanning speed and accurate scanning even the scanning codes are worn, damaged, or twisted.


The above are the strong points of the mobile computer scanner DT 40. Moreover, having met the IP67 standard, and passed the 1.5 m drop test, the mobile computer DT 40 with dust-proof, waterproof and protective function is proved to be a rugged mobile computer. The device and data can be well managed by U-Home software developed by Urovo.


Wide applications are covered. The Urovo DT 40 is especially suitable to be applied in store operation for receiving goods, warehouse management, products management, shopping guide, etc. A large capacity battery of 4500mAh delivers working hours 8 to 10 hours. To explore more about Urovo DT 40, please visit the product introduction for more.




Let’s take a look at other brands' mobile computers. We can see the following picture, a mobile computer with a keypad that is from the other brand. Now let’s see what their differences are.



A chart would be presenting their distinctions. 




From the chart, we can see that the Urovo DT 40 has Android 9.0, a more advanced operating system. And the xxx mobile computer has dual systems, Microsoft Window 6.5 and Android 4.1. However, they are not as stable and powerful as the Android 9.0 version. Talking about the internet, the Urovo DT 40 supports WiFi, Bluetooth, and NFC, while the xxx brand does not support NFC function. As in modern society, NFC has become a more convenient way to scan and collect information, etc. Urovo DT 40 is considered to be a rugged mobile computer scanner passing the IP67 and 1.5m drop test. Compared to Urovo DT 40, the xxx mobile computer also passed the 1.5-meter drop test, but to the water and dust prevention, it only gained IP48. Speaking of the service time, a large capacity of 4500mAh battery was installed, supporting 8-10 hours operation. And the capacity of the xxx brand is only 3900mAh. Concerning the memory, the xxx brand owns 1G RAM+16G ROM, a removable memory card for 32GB is available, while the Urovo possesses 2G RAM+16G ROM, with an optional 2GB+32GB version to choose from. Clearly, Urovo DT 40 outweighs the xxx brand mobile computer.  


Urovo DT 50--A powerful and flexible hand-held mobile computer


Urovo DT 50 is our featured product. Deployed with a powerful Octa-core CPU and operation system, the DT 50 is a portable barcode scanner that equips an international professional 2D scanning engine. With Urovo’s powerful mobile computer DT 50, you get more value out of your mobile computers by adding functionality as well as simplifying deployment and management of your mobile devices, driving productivity and device control to a new level.



What Advantages Does the Urovo DT 50 Possess?


Urovo DT 50, a high-end personal digital assistant at Urovo features an advanced CPU, except that, a super large screen of 5.7-inch is collocated, allowing users to view more information at a glance and manage data more conveniently. Here are the following benefits that the portable barcode reader owns.


1. High-end configuration- An Octa-core CPU and an advanced Android 9.0 lay a solid foundation for a strong performance that enables the device to operate fast and efficiently while consuming less energy.


2. Rugged protection-Meeting with the IP67 standard and 1.5 m drop test passed, dust-proof and waterproof function escalated to a new level.


3. High pixel camera-Pixel of the camera reaches 16 million. A 16 MP color camera for high-definition images or videos gives you visual comfort to a new level.


4. Fast speed scanning-International professional scanning engine offers a fast scanning speed.


5. Super high definition large display-A high sensitivity 5.7-inch display that is easy to read in direct sunlight to the first-time every-time capture of barcodes, labels and documents.


6. Abundant accessories-Fingerprint battery cover, single cradle, sharing cradle, pistol grip and hand strap, makes it a portable barcode scanner for various working environments.


Application of the Urovo DT 50


The application of the portable Urovo DT 50 covers extensive industries such as supply chain warehouse management, logistics, express delivery management, terminal cargo management, and store operation, etc. In the process of management, the portable barcode scanner has realized goods and duty traceable and visible. The necessary information can be well recorded and managed by the DT 50.



Now let’s take a look at other brands' mobile computers. The following picture is the same type of mobile computer as Urovo DT 50 and we name it SSS.



As the same step before, we will list a chart to show their differences, which will be more direct and clear.




Now we can see that Urovo DT 50 possesses a 2.2 GHz processor, meaning higher efficiency in dealing with data than the 1.2 GHz CPU of SSS. About the operation system, the SSS has a version of Android 7.1.1; however, Urovo DT 50 owns a more advanced Android 9.0. Talking about the resolution of the camera, the dual-camera design is embodied in Urovo DT 50, with a front camera 5MP and a rear camera 16MP, providing ultra-high-resolution photos and videos. The 16MP color rear camera captures highly detailed photos and videos-ideal for proof-of-condition/delivery/service, onsite technical support and more. Both the mobile computers configure quick charge design, but the charging time of the Urovo DT 50 is shorter than the SSS. Moreover, a 5.7-inch large display enables more convenient operation. From the above description, we can see that Urovo DT 50 would be a better selection for you.
