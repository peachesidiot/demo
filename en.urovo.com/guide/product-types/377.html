UROVO Retail Solution for Apparel and Footwear Retailers

2022-05-31 14:45:00
Source：Urovo

“Seamlessly Integrate Online & In-Store Operation”


Unlocking the True Value of Retail


The retail transformation has placed a variety of challenges

and demands on apparel and footwear retail. To make the right decisions,

retailers need to rethink their approach to improve the speed and efficiency of

their workflows.


That’s why mobile technologies are here to satisfy every

soaring need. Being popular in retail store management, UROVO has foreseen

significant changes so as to provide a great digital solution for global

merchants.

UROVO retail solution helps apparel and

footwear retail meet the challenges, enabling retailers to improve their mobile

capabilities to provide a better customer experience and achieve better

outcomes.



1. Inventory Counting Management

Help Ensure Accuracy at Every Step of Inventory Counting


Accurately maintaining proper inventory levels, minimizing human errors, and eliminating out-of-stocks are real challenges. But with UROVO, you can leverage technology to improve inventory counting accuracy and staff efficiency.



Get Inventory Counting Right, Right Away


To accurately track products in your warehouse or stockroom

and acquire data when needed, you need the right process and the right tools.

When you need those support, UROVO can help.


UROVO retail solution that is built with a connected

ecosystem of scanners and digital management terminal, which gives apparel and

shoe retailers the performance edge they need to conduct accurate and proficient

inventory counting.




1. Accurately Inventory Counting


Routine tasks like counting inventory and data entry can be a quick one-step process with barcode and RIFD scanners. These Personal Digital Assistants speed up the manual processes and avoid the situation of human error and omission, which in turn greatly improves the accuracy rate of inventory.


2. Improve Inventory Visibility


PDAs allow an organization to shift towards paperless operations. Scanning the same information with mobile computers takes a fraction of the time, freeing hands and making the inventory process more convenient and efficient.


3. Keep the Process Simple


PDAs and or RFID ultra-high frequency devices streamline the daily tasks of an organization such as scanning codes, conducting batch inventory, accurately grasping the inventory quantity, adjusting the sales plan, and avoiding the backlog of outdated goods.




2. Smart In-Store Operation

Rebooting the Apparel and Shoes Store with Digital Operation


There are a wide variety of in-store operation systems that exist, from simple paper to advanced rugged mobile computer technology. You can’t go wrong with a real-time digital solution as it is smart and affordable when used properly.


UROVO’s best-in-class retail technologies include mobile devices, scanners, and digital systems that seamlessly connect to empower workers with everything they need for in-store operation.







Rediscover In-Store Potential, Receive Better Outcome


If you are looking for a way to truly optimize retail store

operation to the best possible, then it will be better to appreciate the

importance of a smart in-store operation system to your business.


UROVO’s retail solution is unlocking the true potential of

the in-store operation. By enabling operators to manage all activities

digitally and in real-time, the vision to be better and more efficient is within

touch.



1. Smarter In-Store Operation


PDAs enable front-line associates to conduct inventory, price checks, scan coupons and communicate with the back warehouses, all while being present with the customer. These low-cost centralized devices allow employees to locate merchandise with ease, improve inventory accuracy and reduce out-of-stock chances. Moreover, If local stores are out of stock, you can use omnichannel inquiry and other store inquiries to achieve goods allocation and other needs.


2. Gain More Marketing Insight


Mobile computers offer many versatile tools and methods for collecting data from consumers on the go. By dynamically recording the status of inventory materials and allowing constant communication with staff, these devices present unprecedented opportunities for a company to learn about its target audience and customize its marketing strategies to respond to customer needs.




3. Elevate Customer Experience

To Offer a Better, Faster, and More Convenience Purchasing Experience


From the moment your customer steps into your shop to the moment they leave with a purchase, you need to check all the boxes of customer satisfaction for them to come back again in the future.


UROVO’s innovative solutions like the handheld barcode scanner help workers provide accurate and reliable product information to customers, scan bars codes swiftly, and make the process of the final transaction quick and easy.



Cast A Spell On Your Shoppers


Once you get customers to walk into

your shop, you need to make sure they leave satisfied, with a purchase, and

with the intention to return. To do that, you need to upgrade your customer

service with modern methods and techniques.


UROVO’s retail solutions like mobile

computers dramatically improve the purchasing process by allowing employees to

update customers about the real-time availability of products and help them in

the decision-making process.



1. Improve Customer Engagement


Customer engagement has become critically important in consumer purchase decisions. It is determined largely by the experience customers have when they purchase products, look for product information, or seek support.


By incorporating RFID scanner, employees can quickly scan codes, check product inventory shelf position in real-time, and respond quickly to customer queries – in turn improving the store shopping experience and enhancing brand image.


2. Shaping Your Brand

The way you serve your customers shapes your brand identity. When you manage to mitigate every customer pain point, whether it's by answering all their questions about the product or scanning their product codes accurately, you create a positive and friendly brand image.
