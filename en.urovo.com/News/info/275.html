Urovo Tech 2016 Channel Partnership Summit: A Decade of Wonders and More

2016-04-09 13:52:00
Source：UROVO


(April 9, 2016, Guizhou, China) Urovo Tech (stock code: 300531), a leading industry application solution provider in the global market, held the 2016 Channel Partnership Summit in Guizhou.

In 2016, Urovo Tech celebrated its 10th anniversary, which was truly a momentous occasion. This young enterprise, which envisions leading the industry in mobile applications, takes pride in serving the information industry for a full decade in the rumbling wheel of times. From real-time reading and writing of data recognition, collection, statistics, and management, to the use of interconnected big data technology, Urovo Tech has successfully driven the development of the mobile application industry chain to meet the increasing data demands of users over the last decade.

In 2016, in light of the deep integration and transformation across industries amid the rapid development of “Internet+”, Urovo Tech seized a window of opportunity to leap forward. To deepen our understanding of the industry development trend and clarify our future development blueprint, Urovo Tech solemnly held the 2016 Channel Partnership Summit in Kaili City, Guizhou on March 30, attracting nearly 100 partners from a pool of industrial elites across the country. The Summit with the theme of “A Decade of Wonders and More” was held in Kaili, Guizhou, where the attendants can appreciate customs of the Miao ethnic group. Integrated with business meetings, leisure tourism, and charity campaigns, this event presents the family of industrial partners with an interactive platform of exchange for deepening industrial understanding.



A Group Photo of Urovo Tech 2016 Channel Partnership Summit

● Channel Partnership Summit: Urovo Tech Shares A Decade of Experiences

On the morning of March 30, Urovo Tech 2016 Channel Partnership Summit was held at Longdu Jingyi International Hotel in Kaili City. The Summit was comprised of the main theme of meeting and industry-specific sidelines, allowing the channel partners to develop a clearer understanding of the business direction of Urovo Tech and engage in a more accurate discussion of industrial trends. During the meeting, Urovo Tech reviewed its previous decade of development and looked forward to future development by clearly delineating plans and supportive policies, including  marketing strategies, empowerment of the channel partner scheme, and the market brand promotion program.

Opening Remarks by Mr. Guo Song, Founder of Urovo Tech

Mr. Guo Song, by taking into account the future direction of Urovo Tech, presented an accurate analysis over the industrial development, which provided for a clear overview of the common goal of partnership advancement in the future. Mr. Guo believed that in light of the deep integration and transformation across industries amid the rapid development of “Internet+” at this stage, as a leading mobile application solutions and service provider in the industry, we are required to bravely innovate and leap forward, timely seize opportunities, and accurately press forward. Urovo Tech, especially in the midst of rising internet finance, will be much better positioned to rumble through the mobile payment sector with a more comprehensive payment product line.

At the same time, Mr. Guo wished this Summit a great success and expressed his gratitude to the partners for their ongoing support. At this Summit, he also looked forward to building a comprehensive platform of in-depth exchanges for partners to analyze the trajectory of product and industrial development, identify business development goals, and join hands to pursue progress and mutual benefits.

Urovo Tech 2016 Channel Partnership Summit

Driven by the fast growing internet finance in 2016, the landscape of the mobile payment sector starts to look up. In assisting our partners in better understanding the digital payment market and industry, Urovo Tech shared its successful experiences in smart POS products and services in the digital payment field. Our application cases include retail chains, garment industry, catering services, commercial real estate, express logistics, and more, demonstrating the great value and benefits brought by Urovo Tech’s smart POS products across industries.

Urovo Tech’s 10th Anniversary Special: Initial Aspiration Continues On

In 2016, Urovo Tech celebrated its 10th anniversary. The 10th Anniversary Special built a strong bridge between Urovo members and partners, allowing them to review past achievements and explore the infinite future.

Urovo Tech’s 10th Anniversary Special

Urovo Tech has witnessed a decade of growth from its inception in 2006, as if it has morphed from a young seedling into a sturdy and fruitful tree with well-developed roots. This transformation is inseparably attributed by the partners who provide us with “sunshine” and “nutrients”. At the 2016 Channel Partnership Summit, which also celebrated the 10th anniversary, Urovo Tech expressed its heartfelt gratitude to partners who have made outstanding contributions and provided encouragement. Among them, a total of 11 partners were awarded commemorative trophies carefully selected by Urovo Tech.

Awarding Ceremony at Urovo Tech’s 2016 Channel Partnership Summit

Following the conclusion of the main theme summit, representatives of various award-winning companies were invited for Urovo Tech’s special interview headed “A Decade of Wonders and More” on the stage. This special interview was well represented by a warm ambience, tugging at the heartstrings of the audience. The stalwart partners expressed their appreciation for the help and support given by Urovo Tech over the years, as well as their confidence and expectations for the future development of joint collaboration.

Urovo Tech and Its Partners Weather the Storm for Mutual Benefits

Urovo Tech Takes the Lead in a Clash of Intriguing Industrial Thoughts

Following the main theme meeting where closely connected and multilayered analyses were presented, the sidelines on the afternoon of March 30 appeared to focus on a clash of intriguing industrial thoughts. As guest speakers, our partners across the country delivered speeches imbued with richness and amazement to the audience, shared classic industry cases, and explored the joint direction and goal of business growth.

Wonderful Glimpse into A Clash of Intriguing Industrial Thoughts

Amid a clash of intriguing industrial thoughts, the Summit also staged an exciting limited-time purchase event. Supported by payment services, such as Urovo Tech’s i9000S payment device and WeChat, the attending partners lined up to place limited-time orders by swiping their cards under the guidance of the Urovo employees on site. Resembling a shopping spree, this event received rave reviews.

Astounding Shopping Spree on Site

● Sojourn to the Miao Tribe: Immersed in the Picturesque Miao Tribe with a Taste of Classic Beauty in Time

After the successful conclusion of the Summit on March 30, Urovo Tech’s channel partners and Urovo team embarked on an exotic sojourn to the Miao Tribe. On the same day, the entire group drove to the most renowned Xijiang Miao Tribe. During this time, the entire group appreciated the uniquely scattered picturesque foothills of Miaoling Mountains, which are surrounded by clear water and streaks. Adventuring into an ancient tribe of mysteries and wonder, Urovo Tech and its partners, through communications and cooperation, not only relaxed their minds and bodies, but also cultivated a precious friendship.

Sitting side by side at the rich bustling Miao long table banquet, Urovo Tech and its partners together toasted the 10th anniversary of Urovo Tech, cherishing this moment of joy and wishing for a brighter future and a greater milestone.

An Eventful Sojourn to the Miao Tribe

Initial Aspiration: Love and Care Lives On

On the afternoon of March 31, Urovo Tech and its partners visited Xijiang Miao Tribe Primary School with the warmest gifts to the humble Miao Tribe children. For this charity event, Urovo Tech donated RMB 11,000 to Xijiang Miao Tribe Primary School, and representatives of various partners gave the children exquisite stationery and books. To recognize this charitable act, Xijiang Miao Tribe Primary School bestowed charity banners on Urovo Tech and its partners.

Urovo Tech and Its Partners Gave Warm Gifts

With the channel partnership summit successfully concluded, as well as a wonderful sojourn to the Miao Tribe for a charity campaign, Urovo Tech’s 2016 Channel Partnership Summit was successfully concluded. Urovo Tech and its partners further deepened their understanding of the current industrial overview and development, cemented their precious friendship, and secured great opportunities for future collaboration.

In the future of the Internet of Things, Urovo Tech wishes to run as a respected enterprise, which not only caters to the needs of users using improved products and services with a forward-looking vision, but also strives to be the best employer that strengthens the wellbeing of the society. At this moment, we are grateful for the right choice made at the beginning of our business, and honored for the strength Urovo Tech has harnessed in this industry. In the next decade, we shall join hands to strengthen our faith in our cause, persist in technological innovation, and commit to the charity and love campaign. Everything in the past became the prologue, and only those who stay on the journey will compose new chapters full of excitement.

Urovo Tech’s “A Decade of Wonders and More” in 2016
