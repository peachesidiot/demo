How to Choose The Best Mobile
Computer for Your Business?

What are mobile computers?

Mobile or handheld computers are portable electronic devices designed for on-the-go data collection and processing. They come in various forms like smartphones, tablets, and PDAs. Mobile computers often have features like wireless connectivity and operating systems that enable efficient barcode scanning and data management.

What are the benefits of using mobile computers?

Mobile computers improve productivity, efficiency, and real-time decision making. Their rugged design ensures durability and a lower total cost of ownership. Wireless capabilities allow seamless data collection and management. At Urovo, our versatile offerings aim to optimize operations for every business.

What industries use mobile computers?

Mobile computers are essential across healthcare, logistics, retail, and manufacturing. In healthcare, they streamline patient identification and care. In logistics, they enable shipment tracking and inventory management. Retailers use them for POS transactions, inventory control, and customer service. Manufacturers leverage them to reduce defects and bottlenecks.

How do you choose the right mobile computer?

Consider your specific industry and use cases. Prioritize battery life and ruggedness for durability. Look for advanced connectivity like Wi-Fi 6E or 5G. Evaluate compatibility with accessories and software needs. Focus on total cost of ownership. Choose a reputable brand for quality and reliability. Assessing these factors will lead to the ideal mobile computer for your business.

What types of barcodes can Urovo devices scan?

Urovo mobile computers can scan both 1D and 2D barcodes, even if damaged or poorly printed. The advanced scanning engines can read codes displayed on screens or held at long ranges.

How durable and waterproof are the devices?

Urovo mobile computers meet military-grade and IP ratings for dust, water, and shock protection. They withstand accidental drops from 1.5-1.8 meters onto concrete.

How long does the battery last?

With swappable batteries, Urovo devices can operate continuously. Large capacity batteries provide 10+ hours of use. Special models can even work in freezing temperatures.

What accessories are available?

Urovo offers charging cradles, cables, cases, holsters, straps, magnetic strips, mounts, extended batteries, snap-on triggers, and more.

What support and services are included?

Urovo provides deployment assistance including but not limited to repairs, app development, training and complete solutions globally.
